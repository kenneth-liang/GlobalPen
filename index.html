Worldwide Pen


With a click of a button you can start your journey in selling pens. As you sell more and more pens, the story will unfold in front of you.
Win: Pen World Ownership = 100% (1,000,000 Pens)

Game Automatically Saves Every 50 Seconds

Game Reloads Saved Data Automatically At Each Site Visit

Getting Started:

Starting by selling a couple of pens to get your business going. Once you have enough money hire your first employee!

Upgrades:

When available, upgrades can be purchased to elevate your business

Automation:

Your Sales Team will automatically click(sell) pens for you. The larger the better, but be aware of the cost!

Appendix:

Business
  • Funds - The only exchange metric of this game. Used to purchase upgrades, materials, hire workers, etc
  • Price Per Pen - Like it sounds. You can lower or raise your prices (by $0.01) with the buttons
  • Marketing - Leveled metric, increasing drastically affects public demand of your pens and allows for increased price point sale
Materials
  • Materials - Primary resource used to create Pens. 1 material = 1 pen created
  • Buying Materials - Using the button you can purchase materials at cost
  • Cost of Materials - The price of materials fluctuates. Red: above average cost. Green: at or below average cost
  • AutoBuy - When toggled, materials will be purchased automatically at current cost
Sales Team
  • Workforce - Number of employees hired. Will help you sell pens
  • Fleet - Number of drones deployed. Will help you sell pens at higher rate
Statistics
  • Public Demand - Determines how attactice your pens are to the public. Reflects low you have to decrease your prices to maintain a positive revenue stream.
  • Unsold Inventory - Number of pens you have made which exceed demand. Offload by offering a sale

Targis:

Targis is your friendly asistant in this game. Responsible for:
  • Communicating current game status changes
  • Flashing upgrades
  • Displaying game progress (Pen World Ownership)
  • Notifying when to decrease Price per Pen (generating 0 sales)
